* Question: $$0.1 + 0.02 + 0.003 + 0.0004 + ... = ?$$ #Math #Sequence
:PROPERTIES:
:collapsed: true
:END:
** Solution:
:PROPERTIES:
:collapsed: true
:END:
*** Let S = 0.1 + 0.02 + 0.003 + 0.0004 + ...
*** 0.1 * S = 0.01 + 0.002 + 0.0003 + ...
*** S - 0.1 * S = 0.1 + 0.01 + 0.001 + 0.0001 + ...
*** 0.9S = 0.11111....
*** Converting repeating decimal to fractions
:PROPERTIES:
:collapsed: true
:END:
**** Let X = 0.11111...
**** 10 * X = 1.11111...
**** 10 * X - X = 1
**** X = 1 / 9
*** Hence, 0.9S = 1/9
*** S = 1/9 * 10/9 = 10 / 81
